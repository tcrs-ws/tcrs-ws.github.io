## Recommended Technical Resources

- [Reactor Model of Computation](https://reactor-model.org/)  
  A high-level overview of the reactor model, which structures event-driven systems around logical time, causality, and deterministic concurrency. The site also highlights major ecosystem projects and events, including material directly related to TCRS and neighboring research communities.

- [Lingua Franca](https://www.lf-lang.org/)  
  The official site for the Lingua Franca coordination language and toolchain for building predictable, concurrent, time-sensitive, and distributed systems. It includes documentation, installation guides, research references, and examples that are particularly useful for attendees working on time-centric reactive software.

- [ReCPS 2026—DATE Workshop W01](https://www.date-conference.com/workshop/w01)  
  The conference page for **Reactive CPS (ReCPS)**, the workshop on reactive cyber-physical systems: design, simulation, and coordination. The program focuses on predictability and composable concurrency for CPS, including reactor-based models of computation and Lingua Franca. ReCPS 2026 is co-located with the Design, Automation and Test in Europe (DATE) Conference in Verona, Italy (20–22 April 2026). Additional workshop information appears on [reactor-model.org (ReCPS 2026)](https://reactor-model.org/events/recps-2026/).

- [Xronos](https://www.xronos.com/)  
  Xronos provides open, component-based software infrastructure for building and operating AI-driven robotics and mobility systems, with emphasis on deterministic behavior from simulation through deployment, integrated telemetry and tracing, and architecture visualization. It complements reactor- and LF-oriented research with tooling oriented toward real-world integration and scale.
